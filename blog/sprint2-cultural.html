~ Core Values ~
a conversation with myself.

Tenacity

Strive for growth to get where you want.

Even if it's frustrating and means long hours

Acknowledge that it’s making you who you need to be; who you want to be.

I've always enjoyed getting punched in the face

A new breaking point always lead me to a new basis. Levelling up mentally or physically I guess you could say.

Limitation

However, sometimes I've found myself saying no to a lot of the things I should wanted to say yes to.

Comprimising my own mental and physical wellbeing in pursuit of an end result.

Integrity

Stay true to you yourself and your morals.

Don’t seek admiration, seek inspiration.

Don't put others down or play games.

Give credit where it's due.

Live your truth. What I've found, is those that see you upholding your values will acknowledge your sincerity and most often they’ll respect you for it.

Don't be afraid to stand up for yourself or others.

Limitation

I've found that having strong morals can be testing on the ego. But then again, I also believe in karma.

Honesty & Transparency

Speak the truth, nobody is a mind reader.

Seek feedback, nothing will better you or an idea than a different perspective.

Ask for help.

Hold yourself accountable

Acknowledge what is happening and what you’re doing. The outcomes will speak for themselves whether or not you’re being truthful.

I've always found asking for help very difficult. I'm relearning to communicate when I need a hand.

Innovation

Seek the Why not the What

Strive to remake and refresh

Respect the value of time

No idea cannot be improved, but sometimes finished is better than perfect.

Trust there is always room to grow, don’t pigeonhole yourself.

Don't be blinded by your first idea.

Family

Family is everything to me.

It's harder to be my friend than to be a part of my family

Collaboration

Help those when you can, not because you want something.

Share ideas, share feedback, share love.

Acknowledge others, their successes, their failures. Who they are, who they’re growing to be.

Those who share your values, they’ll be your true friends. You’ll bounce off each other. Lifes too short not to share that with other people.

Sometimes people aren't going to contribute back, and that's ok too.

Limitation

Sometimes collaboration is difficult, it's lead me to a lot of lonely 2am hours in front of a computer. However, when you're apart of a team where there is trust and contribution. That's my favourite kind of project to be a part of.

Zealousness

Do what you love and love what you do.

Give your life meaning through your true passions.

It will emanate and reflect back through your aura, through your work, through everything you produce whether it’s creative or not.

Love life. Every bit of it.

Humbleness

Acknowledge the fruits of your success but be grateful for all that you have received

Your own pride and purpose is the only validation that you need.

Confidence is measured by your own self worth, not through other peoples reflections of who they think you are. This is something I still practicing everyday.

If you’re happy with yourself that’s gonna show. If you care about what others think about you, that’s gonna show.

Limitation

Let go of your ego, is it hurting you? Or is it just hurting your pride?

A lot of times I wish I had said something...and a lot of times I wish I had kept my mouth shut.

Acceptance

Life goes on.

Reflection is good.

Rumination is painful.

Attitude is everything.
